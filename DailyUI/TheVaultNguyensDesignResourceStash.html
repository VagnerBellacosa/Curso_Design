The Vault - Nguyen's Design Resource stash
A collection of articles & resources that have shaped me as a designer over the years. Hopefully they can help you too. This is not just a compilation of the latest links and trends which are everywhere. But articles and resources that were the most transformative for my career and business, based on key areas of design.
The focus of this resources is to help increase your value as a designer, and to serve as a resource that you can keep referring back to. I hope you get value out of my design resource stash and that it serves you well in 2022. 15+ years in the design industry and I'm still trying to learn and improve in various ways. Keep learning and growing, this industry stops for no one.
